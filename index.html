About Me

Get to know more about my journey

Hello! I'm Asif Mujibur Rahman, a passionate Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence student currently pursuing my 3rd year B.E. in Computer Science & Engineering at St. Joseph's College of Engineering, Chennai, India.

My career objective is to secure a challenging position where I can efficiently contribute my skills and abilities to the growth of the organization while building my professional career. I am eager to learn the maximum about every domain I am involved in, not just a little, and continuously expand my knowledge and experience.

I have completed multiple internships including Python Development at CODSOFT, Web Development at Prodigy Infotech, and Software Debugging at ES Technocorp. My interests include Generative AI & NLP, Agentic AI Frameworks, Deep Learning and LLMs, and Quantum Mechanics.

Born in the Kingdom of Bahrain and fluent in Tamil, English, and Hindi, I bring a diverse perspective to my work. Beyond technical skills, I'm proficient in Microsoft Office, project management, AI tools, and possess effective communication skills.
